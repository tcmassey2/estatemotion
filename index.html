Questions

The honest answers

What if a scene looks wrong?

AI video isn't perfect — occasionally a scene needs a redo. You review every scene before publishing, and regenerating any scene is free. Risky rooms like kitchens and baths render conservatively by design to stay true to your photo.

Is it really my photos, or AI-invented rooms?

Your photos. Vistalia animates the actual images you upload — it doesn't invent rooms or features. Fidelity guardrails keep fixtures and finishes true to what you shot.

Is there a subscription?

No. You buy videos when you list — $100 for one, or less per video in a pack — and credits never expire. Your first video is free, no card required.

How long does a render take?

About three minutes depending on photo count. You approve every scene, then the finished MP4 appears with a download button.
