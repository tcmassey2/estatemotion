How it works

Three steps. Three minutes. Done.

01 — Upload

Drop in your MLS photos

10–20 photos from your photographer, your phone, or the MLS export. We handle JPEG, HEIC, and PNG. No specific size requirements.

02 — Direct

AI plans the cinematography

Our Motion Director analyzes each photo, picks scene order, writes camera moves, and selects transitions based on what's actually in the frame — never invented features.

03 — Render

Download your finished MP4

9:16 vertical for Instagram and TikTok, plus 1:1 and 16:9. Branded outro with your photo and contact details.
