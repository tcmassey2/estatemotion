Questions

The honest answers

What if a scene looks wrong?

AI video isn't perfect — occasionally a scene needs a redo. That's why you review every scene before publishing, and regenerating any scene is free. Nothing goes out that you haven't approved. Risky rooms like kitchens and baths render conservatively by design to stay true to your photo.

Is it really my photos, or AI-invented rooms?

Your photos. EstateMotion animates the actual images you upload — it doesn't invent rooms or features. Fidelity guardrails keep fixtures, counters, and finishes true to what you shot.

Is there a subscription?

No. You buy videos when you list — $100 for one, or less per video in a pack — and credits never expire. Your first video is free, no card required.

Do I need any editing skill?

None. Upload, pick a style, review. The whole thing takes about three minutes and you get a finished, ready-to-post video.
