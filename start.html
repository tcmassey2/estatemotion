Questions

The honest answers

What if a scene looks wrong?

AI video isn't perfect — occasionally a scene needs a redo. That's why you review every scene before publishing, and regenerating any scene is free. Nothing goes out that you haven't approved. Risky rooms like kitchens and baths are rendered conservatively by design to keep them true to the photo.

Is it really my photos, or AI-invented rooms?

Your photos. EstateMotion animates the actual images you upload — it doesn't invent rooms or features. Fidelity guardrails keep fixtures, counters, and finishes true to what you shot.

Do I need any editing skill?

None. Upload, pick a style, review. The whole thing takes about three minutes and you get a finished, ready-to-post video.

What does the free video include?

A complete cinematic listing video from your photos — full length, your branding, all social formats. No credit card to try it.
