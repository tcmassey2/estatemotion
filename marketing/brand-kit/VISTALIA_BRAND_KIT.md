# Vistalia brand kit (editable source — July 2026, q7)

**Vistalia** (pronounced **vih-STAH-lee-ah**, like "Italia") · vistalia.ai ·
legal entity EstateMotion LLC, DBA Vistalia.

**What it is:** turns an agent's listing photos into cinematic vertical (9:16)
video tours — AI camera motion, on-beat music, narration in the agent's own
cloned voice. ~3-minute renders. Built for Reels/TikTok. Audience: US
residential listing agents + small teams.

## Voice & tone
Confident, terse, agent-to-agent. Premium, never precious. Short sentences,
concrete numbers, let the product be the drama.
Sounds like: "Photos in. Video out." · "Your listing, cinematic." · "Narrated
in YOUR voice." · "The coffee took longer."
Never: hype adjectives, exclamation stacking, invented results/testimonials,
naming competitors in ads.

## True facts ads may state (q7)
First video free (no card) · $39 one-off · Pro $69/mo (5 videos) · Studio
$149/mo (10) · annual $490/$990 (41–45% off) · ~3-min renders · 60s tours ·
own-voice cloning · on-beat music · 9:16 vertical.

## Typography
Fraunces (display serif, headlines, tight -0.025em, sentence case) · Inter
(body/UI) · JetBrains Mono (prices/labels). Gold is an accent, never body text.

## Imagery
Aspirational-but-believable US suburban luxury; desert-modern (Scottsdale)
tests best. Twilight/golden hour, warm glowing interiors, glassy pools, staged
rooms, HDR real-estate photography realism, slow cinematic camera moves,
vertical-first. Never: neon/cyberpunk, cartoon/illustration, stock-watermark
look, cold blue corporate, fisheye, empty unstaged rooms.

## Rules for AI-generated ads
1. Faceless by default — the founder never appears (no face, no voice).
2. UGC uses fictional AI personas (JESS/MARCUS/DIANE — marketing/UGC_AD_PROMPTS.md);
   never present them as real customers.
3. No actor ever shows a screen to camera — "watch this" = hard cut to real
   product footage.
4. Spoken brand name is always written "Vih-STAH-lee-ah" in dialogue.
5. Claims limited to the true-facts list. No invented stats/leads/sales.
6. End every ad on the gold-on-dark end card (endcard.png): mark + "Your first
   video is free" + vistalia.ai.
7. Music: Vistalia's licensed Pixabay library or platform-licensed only.
