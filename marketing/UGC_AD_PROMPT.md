# EstateMotion — UGC Ad Master Prompt

Reusable prompt for generating high-retention UGC ads (Higgsfield / Veo /
Kling). Keeps the "hook in 3s, new beat every 1–2s" retention discipline from
the viral-reel templates — but tuned for what actually converts realtors:
authentic, phone-real, product-true. NOT sci-fi VFX (that reads as AI slop and
misrepresents the product).

## The rule that matters most
The middle of every ad should be **real EstateMotion output** (an actual
listing video) or a **real screen-capture of the app**, not generated footage.
Generated footage is only for the talking-head bookends. The proof must be real.

## Master prompt (talking-head UGC hook)

> Authentic UGC selfie video, shot on a front-facing phone camera, vertical
> 9:16. A real-estate agent [DESCRIBE: age, look, setting — e.g. "early-30s
> agent in a blazer, parked in their car in daylight"] talks directly to
> camera with natural, slightly-excited energy, like telling a friend a
> secret. Realistic phone-camera texture, natural lighting, subtle handheld
> movement. Clear spoken dialogue. No music bed, no text overlays, no VFX,
> no studio polish — it should look like a real person filmed it on their
> iPhone.
>
> HOOK (first 3 seconds), she says: "[HOOK LINE]"
> Then: "[ONE-LINE PROOF / how it works]"
> She glances at her phone as if about to show the screen.

Keep it 8–15 seconds. The agent reads as real, not an actor. Then in editing
you cut from her phone to the **real product footage**.

## Hook lines that work (swap one in — test 5+)
- "I stopped paying $200 a video for my listings — watch this."
- "My lender asked who shoots my listing videos. It's an app."
- "This took my listing photos and made *this* in three minutes."
- "Every agent in my office is about to be mad I shared this."
- "POV: your $300K listing now looks like a $1M listing."
- "I uploaded 12 photos and got a cinematic video back. For free."

## Full ad assembly (the structure that retains)
1. **0–3s — Hook:** talking-head UGC (generated or you on camera), one of the lines above.
2. **3–10s — Proof:** cut to REAL EstateMotion footage — the before/after
   (static MLS photo → cinematic video) or a screen-cap of the render. New
   visual beat every 1–2s (scene cuts), but real footage.
3. **10–15s — CTA:** back to the agent, or a clean end card: "Your first
   video's free. Link in bio." Calm, confident.

## Hard rules (compliance + conversion)
- **No fabricated specific claims** in an AI actor's mouth ("I made $X",
  "I sold in Y days") — FTC testimonial risk. Demonstrative framing only.
- **No sci-fi / VFX / holograms / neon.** Real estate, real-feeling.
- **Captions, not baked-in text.** Add them natively in Meta/CapCut + a
  trending sound — it reads as organic and renders crisper.
- **The product shot is always real output**, never generated.

## Variant matrix (mass-produce from this)
Rotate only the hook line + the agent/setting + the property type in the
proof footage. Same skeleton → 10–20 variants/week. Kill any ad over ~$8
cost-per-trial after 3 days; scale the winners.
